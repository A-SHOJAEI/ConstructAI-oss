# Commercial Licensing

ConstructAI is distributed under the [PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0](./LICENSE) license. **Commercial use requires a separate license** from the copyright holder.

## What counts as noncommercial

The following are permitted under the noncommercial license at no cost:

- **Personal use** — research, experiment, learning, hobby projects, evaluation
- **Academic use** — teaching, coursework, student projects, dissertation research
- **Public-interest organizations** — registered charities, public research institutes, environmental protection groups
- **Forks and modifications** for any of the above purposes
- **Contributions back** to this repository

See the [LICENSE](./LICENSE) for the full legal text.

## What requires a commercial license

If your organization (or any project you work on) uses, modifies, or
distributes ConstructAI to:

- Deliver paid services to clients
- Power a SaaS or hosted offering
- Embed in a commercial product
- Generate revenue, directly or indirectly
- Support internal business operations of a for-profit entity

…then you need a commercial license.

## How to get a commercial license

Email **shojaei@vt.edu** with:

1. Your organization name and website
2. A short description of how you intend to use ConstructAI
3. Approximate scale (number of users, number of projects, hosted or on-prem)
4. Whether you want a perpetual license, an annual subscription, or a one-time evaluation extension

We respond within five business days and tailor the agreement to your use
case. Commercial licenses typically include:

- Right to use ConstructAI in commercial contexts
- Indemnification terms
- (Optional) Support and update commitments
- (Optional) Custom feature work
- (Optional) Removal of noncommercial-license attribution requirements


## Questions

Ambiguous about whether your use case is commercial? Just ask — short
