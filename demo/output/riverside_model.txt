IFC Model Placeholder
=====================
Install ifcopenshell to generate actual IFC4 model:
  pip install ifcopenshell

Model specification:
  - 1 IfcSite: Riverside Mixed-Use Development Site
  - 1 IfcBuilding: Main Building
  - 5 IfcBuildingStorey: Levels 1-5
  - 20 IfcWall: 4 per floor (N, S, E, W)
  - 5 IfcSlab: 1 per floor
  - 10 IfcColumn: 2 per floor
  - Total elements: 36
